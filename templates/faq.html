What is an imageboard?

An imageboard is a type of Internet forum that revolves around the posting of images, often alongside text and discussion. The first imageboards were created in Japan as an extension of the textboard concept. These sites later inspired the creation of a number of English-language imageboards, such as 4chan. (source: Wikipedia)

Imageboards tend to be anonymous, meaning that posters don't need to register on the site to post and posts don't usually have names attatched to them.

Why was Saturn made?

Saturn was made as a proof of concept. I(Sen) wanted to see if I could get something close to the level of LynxChan or JSchan using Python. I find these sorts of projects interesting and I hope that this will help someone in some way. I hope that more interesting people start moving away from sites like 4chan and make their own smaller communities. Mainstream social media has gotten so bad that the only way people can stay and "fit in" is by having extremist opinions or just constantly acting like idiots. There's no political statement, no angry manifesto, no other such bullshit. I just find this sort of stuff interesting.
